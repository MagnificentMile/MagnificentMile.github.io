Chinese Proverb Speech "OX"

Chengyu is a type of traditional Chinese idiom, commonly consisting of four characters. Chengyu is widely used in Classical Chinese, and is still common in vernacular Chinese writing and the spoken language today. 2021 is the zodiac year of the "OX", and there is a bunch of chengyu with the characters "OX" in it. This gave kids a chance to share what they knew about the Chengyu they had picked and also gave them a chance to practice their public speaking skills. Come to join us on Jan 23!

About Us

Chicago Chinese Children Club, colloquially abbreviated as CCCC, is an Illinois not for profit corporation whose core mission is, among others, to promote and nurture the Chinese culture and heritage among children in ethnic Chinese families in the Greater Chicago Area.
